What Is The Best Name For A Lifestyle Store?

When it comes to figuring out fashion brand names or a business name for your lifestyle store, the first detail you should iron out is your target audience.
Chalk down the characteristics of the type of consumer who will walk into your lifestyle store, and brainstorm over name ideas that will appeal to them.
While trying to choose a good boutique name, develop a theme that outlines what your brand represents. Once you have a few definite names in mind,
do a test run with a group of people who match your target audience to get their feedback.
